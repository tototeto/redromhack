Fire Red Hack by tototeto

This is a rom hack of Pokemon Fire Red. It has been edited to include more pokemon, toughen gyms and elite four slightly, and make areas much easier to pass through or skip.

The download is for the latest version, currently 1.2.

If you are playing this on a computer and do not have an emulator, you can download Visual Boy Advance Visual Boy Advance or you can download mGBA (Works for both Mac and PC) from themGBA download site.

If you are on an iPhone you can go to this link to download GBA4IOS11 v2.1. For it to work, you will have to go to your setting and trust the app. There is a concise video tutorial on the download site if you need it. iEmulators
